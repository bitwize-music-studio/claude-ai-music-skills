# Nerdcore

## Genre Overview

Nerdcore (also nerdcore hip-hop) is a subgenre of hip-hop music characterized by themes and subject matter considered of interest to nerds. Self-described nerdcore musicians often rap about video games, science fiction, technology, anime, comic books, and other "geeky" topics.

## Characteristics

- **Lyrical Focus**: Technical jargon, pop culture references, gaming, hacking, sci-fi
- **Production**: Ranges from chiptune/8-bit to modern hip-hop beats, often incorporates samples from games/tech
- **Tone**: Can be humorous, braggadocious (about nerd cred), introspective, or socially conscious
- **Community**: Strong DIY ethos, convention circuit, internet-native fanbase
