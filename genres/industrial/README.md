# Industrial

## Genre Overview

Industrial music combines aggressive electronic sounds, mechanical rhythms, and dark atmospheres. Originating in the late 1970s, the genre ranges from harsh noise to danceable EBM. In hip-hop contexts, "industrial" refers to dark, aggressive electronic production with distorted textures and mechanical beats.

## Characteristics

- **Sound Design**: Distorted synths, harsh textures, mechanical sounds, glitchy elements
- **Drums**: Aggressive, punchy, often distorted; TR-808/909 with heavy processing
- **Atmosphere**: Dark, menacing, dystopian, oppressive
- **Production**: Heavy compression, distortion, layered noise, sub-bass
- **Mood**: Aggressive, paranoid, claustrophobic, intense

## Subgenres & Styles

| Style | Description | Reference Artists |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| Industrial Rock | Guitar-driven, aggressive, mainstream crossover | Nine Inch Nails, Ministry, KMFDM |
| EBM (Electronic Body Music) | Danceable, sequenced, club-oriented | Front 242, Nitzer Ebb, DAF |
| Darkwave | Atmospheric, gothic, synth-driven | Clan of Xymox, She Past Away |
| Industrial Hip-Hop | Dark electronic beats with rap vocals | Death Grips, clipping., Dälek |
| Aggrotech | Harsh, aggressive EBM, distorted vocals | Combichrist, Hocico |
