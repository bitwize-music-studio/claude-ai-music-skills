## Reference Tracks

### Daft Punk
- "Da Funk" (Homework, 1997) — Gritty filtered synth riff; driving house beat; lo-fi yet funky
- "Around the World" (Homework, 1997) — Minimalist repetition as euphoria; pulsing bassline
- "One More Time" (Discovery, 2001) — Euphoric compressed vocals over chopped disco sample
- "Digital Love" (Discovery, 2001) — Romantic vocoder pop on George Duke sample
- "Harder Better Faster Stronger" (Discovery, 2001) — Vocoder as lead instrument; Grammy-winning live version
- "Something About Us" (Discovery, 2001) — Intimate vocoder love ballad
- "Get Lucky" (Random Access Memories, 2013) — Live disco with Nile Rodgers guitar and Pharrell vocals; five Grammys
- "Instant Crush" (Random Access Memories, 2013) — Julian Casablancas vocals through vocoder over disco-rock
