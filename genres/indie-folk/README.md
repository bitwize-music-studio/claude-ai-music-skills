# Indie Folk

## Genre Overview

Indie folk blends traditional folk music with indie rock sensibilities. Characterized by acoustic instrumentation, intimate vocals, and introspective storytelling, the genre emphasizes authenticity and emotional honesty over polished production.

## Characteristics

- **Instrumentation**: Acoustic guitar, piano, light percussion, strings, banjo, mandolin
- **Vocals**: Warm, intimate, often hushed or breathy delivery
- **Production**: Organic, warm, sometimes lo-fi with room ambiance
- **Tone**: Contemplative, cozy, nostalgic, bittersweet, hopeful
- **Themes**: Love, memory, home, nature, quiet moments, personal reflection

## Subgenres & Styles

| Style | Description | Reference Artists |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| Chamber Folk | Orchestral elements, strings, lush arrangements | Fleet Foxes, Bon Iver |
| Lo-fi Folk | Raw, intimate, minimal production | Elliott Smith, Sufjan Stevens |
| Coffee Shop | Warm, cozy, perfect for cafes | Iron & Wine, José González |
| Americana Folk | Country-tinged, storytelling | The Lumineers, Brandi Carlile |
