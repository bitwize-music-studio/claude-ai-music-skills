# Metal

## Genre Overview

Metal is a high-energy, guitar-driven genre characterized by distorted electric guitars, powerful vocals, aggressive drums, and often symphonic or dark atmospheric production. From the blues-rock foundations of heavy metal to the technicality of progressive and the raw aggression of black and death metal, the genre encompasses a wide spectrum of sounds united by intensity, attitude, and musical virtuosity.

## Characteristics

- **Instrumentation**: Distorted electric guitar, bass guitar, drums, often keyboards or orchestral elements
- **Vocals**: Varies from clean to aggressive screams/growls, often melodic within aggressive delivery
- **Production**: Heavy compression, distorted guitars, powerful drums
- **Energy**: High-intensity, performance-driven, technically complex
- **Structure**: Verse-chorus with extended solos, breakdowns, dynamic transitions
- **Themes**: Power, darkness, mythology, social commentary, technical mastery

## Subgenres & Styles

| Style | Description | Reference Artists |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| Heavy Metal | Foundational metal, blues-based, powerful | Black Sabbath, Judas Priest, Motörhead |
| Thrash Metal | Fast, aggressive, complex riffs | Metallica, Slayer, Exodus |
| Death Metal | Extreme vocals, low tunings, technical | Death, Obituary, Cannibal Corpse |
| Black Metal | Raw production, tremolo guitars, atmospheric | Bathory, Burzum, Emperor |
| Doom Metal | Slow, heavy, melancholic, psychedelic | Candlemass, Sleep, Electric Wizard |
| Progressive Metal | Complex song structures, virtuosic | Dream Theater, Opeth, Mastodon |
| Power Metal | Fast, melodic, epic themes | Helloween, Stratovarius, Avantasia |
| Nu-Metal | Groove-oriented, rap influence, modern | Korn, Deftones, System of a Down |
