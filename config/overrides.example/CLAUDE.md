## Example

```markdown
## My Workflow Preferences
- Always ask before creating new albums
- Default to 8-track albums unless specified
- Use dark/moody themes by default

## Album Naming Conventions
- All lowercase with hyphens
- No numbers in album names
- Keep names under 20 characters

## Track Naming Conventions
- Title case for display names
- Lowercase with hyphens for filenames

## Research Preferences
- Prioritize primary sources over news articles
- Always include timeline in RESEARCH.md
- Minimum 3 sources per factual claim

## Generation Preferences
- Generate 3 variations per track
- Stop iterating after 5 attempts
- Prioritize vocal clarity over production polish

## Release Preferences
- Always create promo videos
- Upload to SoundCloud same day as completion
- Wait 1 week before distributor submission
```
