## Example

```markdown
## Source Priority
1. Court documents, SEC filings, government reports
2. Academic research, peer-reviewed journals
3. Investigative journalism (NYT, WSJ, WaPo, ProPublica)
4. Industry publications, trade press

## Verification Standards
- Minimum sources for key facts: 3
- Citation format: Chicago style
- Cross-reference requirement: Yes for all claims

## Research Depth
- Timeline precision: Exact dates when available
- Financial detail level: Dollar amounts to nearest thousand
- Quote requirements: Direct quotes for all attributions

## Trusted Sources
### Always Trust
- PACER court records
- SEC EDGAR filings
- DOJ press releases

### Approach with Caution
- Wikipedia (use only for context, verify claims)
- Social media posts (screenshot and archive)

### Never Use
- Anonymous forum posts without corroboration
- Tabloid sources
- AI-generated content
```
