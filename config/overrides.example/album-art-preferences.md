## Example

```markdown
## Visual Style
### Prefer
- Minimalist, geometric, high contrast
- Abstract representations
- Glitch art, digital aesthetics

### Avoid
- Photorealistic images
- Busy compositions
- Text overlays (except artist name)

## Color Palette
### Primary Colors
- Deep blues (#0a1628)
- Rich purples (#2d1b4e)
- Near black (#0a0a0a)

### Accent Colors
- Neon cyan (#00ffff)
- Electric pink (#ff00ff)

### Avoid Colors
- Warm oranges, yellows
- Pastels
- Earth tones

## Composition
### Prefer
- Centered subject with negative space
- Single focal point
- Symmetry or intentional asymmetry

### Avoid
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Multiple competing elements
- Busy patterns
```
