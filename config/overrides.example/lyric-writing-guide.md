## Example Filled Out

```markdown
## Style Preferences
- Prefer first-person narrative
- Avoid religious imagery
- Use vivid sensory details
- Keep verses 4-6 lines max

## Vocabulary
### Prefer
- Simple, direct language
- Action verbs over state verbs

### Avoid
- utilize, commence, endeavor (too formal)
- Cliches: heart of gold, burning bright

## Themes
### Focus On
- Technology, alienation, urban decay
- Personal transformation

### Avoid
- Love songs, party anthems
- Generic motivational content

## Custom Rules
- Never use the word "baby" in lyrics
- Always end with a callback to the opening line
```
